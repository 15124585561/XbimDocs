IfcRelOccupiesSpaces Class
IfcRelOccupiesSpaces is a relationship class that further constrains the parent relationship IfcRelAssignsToActor to a relationship between occupants (IfcOccupant) and either a space (IfcSpace), a collection of spaces (IfcZone), a building storey (IfcBuildingStorey), or a building (IfcBuilding).
